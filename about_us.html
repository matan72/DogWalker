Why PaWalk?

In this day and age, life is understandably hectic for many pet parents, with commitments at work, time out with friends and family. So whether you are having late nights at work, out on a holiday or simply need a break PaWalk is here to connect you with professional dog walkers who are ready to help you take care of your dogs. PaWalk walkers are normal people who love dogs, just like you. PaWalk walkers also limit the number of dogs that they take for a walk, giving your pet much deserved attention!
